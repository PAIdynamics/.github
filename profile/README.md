# PAI Dynamics

PAI Dynamics builds physics-aware agentic AI systems for fusion energy and space technology. We help research teams and engineering organizations modernize legacy simulation workflows, translate deep domain expertise into autonomous tools, and accelerate the design of complex physics systems.

From Fortran-to-C++ modernization to GPU-ready simulation pipelines and AI-assisted engineering workflows, our goal is simple: make high-performance scientific computing faster, more portable, and easier to use for the next generation of fusion and aerospace innovation.
