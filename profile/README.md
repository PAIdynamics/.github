# PAI Dynamics

PAI Dynamics is a startup building a new paradigm for scientific computing by embedding AI directly into physics-based simulation software 
to enable fast, scalable, and optimization-ready engineering.

We develop agentic AI systems powered by specialized large language models that transform legacy scientific codes into modern, differentiable, and performance-portable platforms. This allows engineers and researchers to run end-to-end optimization, compute gradients through simulations, 
and explore design spaces with unprecedented efficiency.

PAI Dynamics targets industries where accuracy, physical constraints, and limited data make traditional AI insufficient, 
starting with fusion energy and space engineering. By combining differentiable programming, high-performance computing, 
and physics-constrained modeling, we enable optimization over billions of parameters without requiring large training datasets.

Our mission is to accelerate innovation in high-tech engineering by turning simulation software into intelligent, 
adaptive systems, reducing development time from years to months and unlocking entirely new design capabilities.
